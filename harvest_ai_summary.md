# Project Summary: HarvestAI (TssCoder)

## Context
HarvestAI is a sustainable agriculture project aimed at addressing soil degradation in Thailand caused by continuous monoculture farming (cassava, corn, sugarcane). It uses AI to promote crop rotation, planning, and forecasting.

## Core Features
- **Personalized Crop Rotation (LLM)**: Recommends crop plans based on soil health, location, and history.
- **Weather Forecasting (ML)**: Predicts conditions and alerts farmers using ML models and APIs (OpenWeatherMap, FarmWeather).
- **Market Price Prediction (ML)**: Time-series models forecast crop prices to guide profitable planting decisions.
- **Satellite Monitoring**: Uses satellite-based land monitoring for precise data.

## Technical Implementation
- **Frontend**: Web-based application.
- **Backend/Logic**: 
  - LLM integration for personalized advice.
  - ML models for time-series forecasting and classification.
  - Webhook integration for user communication.
- **Inspiration**: EUAgriData Portal.

## Ethics & Impact
- **SDGs**: Aligned with Goals 2 (Zero Hunger), 9 (Industry & Innovation), and 12 (Responsible Consumption).
- **Ethics**: Follows UNESCO Principles (Transparency, Explainability, Privacy).
